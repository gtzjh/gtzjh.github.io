Harbin Engineering University Welcomes Dr. Qin Chao as New Associate Professor

28/8/2024

  • Harbin Engineering University, a preeminent institution (211 Project University) known for its commitment to excellence in engineering and science, announced the appointment of Dr. Qin Chao as an Associate Professor in the College of Aerospace and Civil Engineering.
  • Dr. Qin joined the EASE group last April and is set to begin his new role in September 2024. Big congratulations to Qin Chao.
