Perspective

The Xu Group is constantly seeking motivated and hard working members. Prospective members are encouraged to browse our website, publications, and presentations. We value more about your interests and motivations in research than past research experience. Our research involves a wide range of skills, including plumbing, soldering, coding, data analysis, etc. We strive to match the research projects to the personality and career plan of group members. We welcome applications with diverse backgrounds (i.e., atmospheric science, chemical engineering, mechanical engineering, computer science, chemistry, physics, mathematics, etc). If you are interested in our work, please send an email to xu1@wustl.edu to inquire about openings, funding, and potential research projects.
