Short Bio

Ms. Zhou Runjing - Miss Zhou received her bachelor's degree in Atmospheric Sciences from Sun Yat-sen University and a master's degree in Environmental Science and Management from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. She is currently a PhD student in the EASE group, where her projects focuses on photochemical air pollution using both observation and modeling approaches. Her current project is titled "Rise in Summertime Ozone Levels in South China: Impacts of Long-Range Transport of Southeast Asia Emissions".
