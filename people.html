Dr. Lyu held various positions at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University from September 2017 to January 2023, including Research Assistant, Postdoctoral Fellow, Research Fellow, and Assistant Professor, before joining Hong Kong Baptist University. His career has focused on air quality research, specifically investigating atmospheric reactive organic species and secondary air pollution. To date, Dr. Lyu has published over 70 scientific papers in internationally renowned journals such as One Earth, Environmental Science & Technology, and Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres. His research has garnered over 3100 citations, with an h-index of 30. He has successfully led numerous competitive research grants, including 2 RGC/GRFs, 1 RGC/CRF (co-host), 1 PPR, and 1 NSFC/Youth Science Fund. Dr. Lyu actively contributes to the scientific community as a Young Editor for The Innovation, a member of the Editorial Board for Frontiers in Sustainable Cities, and a member of the Australia-China Air Quality Science and Management Centre.
